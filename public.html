How We Monitor
Each bridge has piezoelectric, vibration (SW420), and flame sensors sending real-time data every 500ms to our cloud system.
🔢
What is SHI?
The Structural Health Index (0–1) is computed by our ML model. Above 0.80 = Healthy. 0.60–0.80 = Monitor. Below 0.60 = Concern.
🆘
Emergency
If a bridge shows UNSAFE, avoid crossing. Report unusual sounds or movements to local authorities immediately.
